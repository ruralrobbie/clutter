Why stewardship matters

Stewardship is caring for what you’ve been given—time, space, and belongings—so they serve your life, not weigh it down.

Actionable Stewardship

Use this five-step loop whenever you declutter:

  1. 1. Set a boundary

    Choose one drawer, one shelf, or 15 minutes.

  2. 2. Sort honestly

    Keep, donate, recycle, or discard—no “maybe” pile.

  3. 3. Return with intention

    Put items where you’ll use them. Store by frequency.

  4. 4. Release responsibly

    Donate what’s usable, recycle what isn’t, and trash the rest.

  5. 5. Maintain lightly

    One in, one out. Review monthly.
